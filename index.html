About Me

Hi! I'm Daniella Embu, a student at St. Olaf College, passionate about knitting and sharing God's love. I started Wrapped In His Love to bring warmth, comfort, and encouragement to others through hand-knit scarves and prayerful, personalized messages. Each scarf is made with care, prayed over, and sent out with a scripture note chosen just for you. My hope is that every package is a reminder that you are deeply loved by God.

Let me help you or someone you love feel wrapped in God's love—one stitch and one prayer at a time.
